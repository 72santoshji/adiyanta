ADIYANTA

यत् सर्वं भवति, पर स्वयं न भवति
“That because of which everything happens, yet which itself does not happen.”

That which exists before the beginning
and remains after the end.

This is not a book.
It is a sitting.

I sat before the fire.
Not to pray.
Not to ask.
But to watch.

I asked myself ten questions.
Not to receive answers.
But to see what survives them.

Prateek Chaudhary

Not a philosopher by training.
Not a seeker by profession.

His work does not explain reality.
It sits with it.

The fire has done its work.

What was false has burned.

What remains does not need light.
